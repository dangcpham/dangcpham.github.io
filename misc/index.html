Undergraduate

The Cornell Mathematical Contest in Modeling is a university-wide competition held over 4 days where a team of 3 undergraduates work together to solve a real-world problem. I have participated in the competition a few times and our team won first place in 2017 (note that the writing quality reflects the limited sleep that this competition affords).
