Gravitational Lensing (work in progress)

Astronomy research at the Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics on gravitational lensing.Conducted data analysis by programming

Work in progress. Anticipating publication

2014-2015

Astronomy - chemistry research on possible exotic diatomic anions’ (such as H2− and N2−)
existence, properties, and potential impacts in stars or interstellar mediums. Guided by Dr. Edward Chen and Herman Keith.

Publication: Hylleraas hydride binding energy: Diatomic electron affinities. Journal of Molecular Modeling (2015) 21:79, (DOI:10.1007/s00894-015-2598-0)
http://link.springer.com/article/10.1007%2Fs00894-015-2598-0
Topic keyword: stellar astrophysics, anions, physical chemistry of diatomic anions.
